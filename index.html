valdbot · command reference

Commands

A Twitch chat bot for MCSR Ranked stats. Use these commands in any channel where ValdBot is active.

+cmd Your own stats (or explicit IGN)
+cmd @user Mentioned user's linked account
#cmd Streamer's linked account
season:N Filter by season number
Stats Records Analysis Account Channel
01 · Stats

Player Stats

📊
+elo  #elo  +elo @user
Current elo, rank, W/L, PB, and average
+

Shows a full snapshot of a player's ranked stats for the current season, including elo rating, peak, rank tier, placement, win/loss record, winrate, matches played, personal best, average completion time, and phase points.

You +elo [IGN] [season:N]
Mention +elo @username [season:N]
Streamer #elo [season:N]
Example output
ValdarenMC Elo: 1482 (1531 Peak) ❚ Emerald III (#214) ❚ W/L: 312/278 (52.9%) ❚ Matches: 601 Played ❚ Pb: 5:47 Average: 9:14 ❚ Phase Points: 88
💡 Phase Points only appear for Season 6 and later. Past seasons are supported via season:N.
🕐
+today  #today  +today @user
Stats from the last 12 hours of play
+

Returns a summary of a player's ranked matches played in the last 12 hours — current elo with net change, win/loss, winrate, and average completion time for that session.

You +today [IGN]
Mention +today @username
Streamer #today
Example output
ValdarenMC 12hr Ranked Stats ❚ Elo: 1482 (+14) ❚ W/L: 8/5 (61.5%) ❚ Average: 9:09
02 · Records

Head-to-Head

⚔️
+record  #record  +record @user
Win/loss record between two players
+

Shows the head-to-head ranked record between two players this season. The first player defaults to your linked account (or the streamer's in # mode). The opponent can be a plain IGN or an @mention. Both can also be given explicitly.

You +record <IGN2> [season:N]
Explicit +record <IGN1> <IGN2> [season:N]
Mention +record @user1 @user2 [season:N]
Streamer #record <IGN> [season:N]
Streamer #record @user [season:N]
Example output
ValdarenMC 1-0 czelco ❚ 1 total game played this season
03 · Analysis

Deep Stats

⏱️
+average  #average  +average @user
Average completion time broken down by seed and bastion type
+

Shows overall average completion time plus per-seed averages (Village, RP, BT, Ship, Temple) and per-bastion averages (Bridge, Treasure, Housing, Stables). Only counts completed (non-forfeited) wins.

You +average [IGN] [season:N]
Mention +average @username [season:N]
Streamer #average [season:N]
Example output
ValdarenMC's overall average: 9:14 (312 completions) ❚ Village: 9:08 ⋮ RP: 9:19 ⋮ BT: 9:11 ⋮ Ship: 9:22 ⋮ Temple: 9:31 ❚ Bridge: 9:12 ⋮ Treasure: 9:09 ⋮ Housing: 9:17 ⋮ Stables: 9:21
📈
+winrate  #winrate  +winrate @user
Winrate broken down by seed and bastion type
+

Shows overall winrate plus per-seed and per-bastion winrates. Draws are excluded from the winrate calculation.

You +winrate [IGN] [season:N]
Mention +winrate @username [season:N]
Streamer #winrate [season:N]
Example output
ValdarenMC's overall winrate: 52.9% (590 matches) ❚ Village: 55.1% ⋮ RP: 51.3% ⋮ BT: 54.8% ⋮ Ship: 48.6% ⋮ Temple: 50.0% ❚ Bridge: 53.4% ⋮ Treasure: 56.2% ⋮ Housing: 49.1% ⋮ Stables: 51.7%
🔁
+last  #last  +last @user
Average and winrate for the last N games
+

Shows average time and winrate (combined per seed and bastion) for the most recent N ranked matches. Useful for seeing recent form rather than season-wide stats.

You +last [IGN] <quantity> [season:N]
Mention +last @username <quantity> [season:N]
Streamer #last <quantity> [season:N]
Example output — +last 50
ValdarenMC's last 50 games: Overall: 9:11 (55.1%) ❚ Village: 9:05 (57.9%) ⋮ RP: 9:14 (52.0%) ⋮ BT: 9:09 (56.3%) ⋮ Ship: 9:24 (50.0%) ⋮ Temple: 9:28 (48.1%) ❚ Bridge: 9:10 (54.2%) ⋮ Treasure: 9:07 (58.3%) ⋮ Housing: 9:16 (51.5%) ⋮ Stables: 9:19 (53.8%)
💡 Quantity is required. If no IGN is given, your linked account is used.
🏳️
+ff  #ff  +ff @user
Forfeit count and rate for the season
+

Shows how many times a player has forfeited this season and what percentage of their total matches that represents.

You +ff [IGN] [season:N]
Mention +ff @username [season:N]
Streamer #ff [season:N]
Example output
ValdarenMC has forfeited 42 (7.0% of matches) times this season
04 · Account

Linking Your Account

🔗
+link
Link your Twitch account to a Minecraft IGN
+

Links your Twitch username to a Minecraft IGN. Once linked, you can use commands without providing your IGN every time — just +elo, +today, etc. The bot verifies the IGN exists via Mojang before saving it.

You +link <IGN>
Example
+link ValdarenMC → Account successfully linked to ValdarenMC!
💡 Your IGN is stored with the correct casing from Mojang. Mention mode (+cmd @user) requires the mentioned user to have linked their account first.
05 · Channel

Bot Management

➕
+join
Invite ValdBot to your channel
+

Makes ValdBot join your Twitch channel so viewers can use all commands there. Run this command from any channel where ValdBot is already active.

You +join
Response
Joined your channel!
➖
+leave
Remove ValdBot from your channel
+

Makes ValdBot leave your channel. You can re-add it at any time with +join.

You +leave
Response
Left your channel.
